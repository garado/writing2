Apostrophe Catastrophe (Part Two)
Grammar Girl
This article deals with how to make words that end in S possessive, as in Kansas’s vs. Kansas’. According to Grammar Girl, either way is perfectly acceptable and which to choose is simply a matter of style. AP style does not add an extra S after the apostrophe, whereas the Chicago style does (with a few very rare exceptions). I wholeheartedly appreciate this article because I have, despite major opposition from everyone and their moms and lots of red-pen corrections from past English teachers, long stood by my choice to add an extra S after the apostrophe. Thank you, Grammar Girl, for reconfirming the validity I always knew my stylistic choices had.
