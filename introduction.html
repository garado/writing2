Hopefully the fact that I finally get some freedom makes the process easier.
Shitty experiences in high school writing classes aside, I've always appreciated the English language and am excited to learn how to command it better in order to produce better writing. I'm also interested in potentially putting a name to all of the unspoken rules of the English language that I as a native English speaker have subtly picked up on, such as that one weird thing about having all adjectives specifically in the order of opinion-size-age-shape-colour-origin-material-purpose-noun.
